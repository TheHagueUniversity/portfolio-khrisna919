Digital Skill

In this project I need to translate the material for design and creativity class digitally. I worked individually. I chose Adobe XD to create an app that consists of the materials. That was my first time using Adobe XD to create a clickable prototype. During the process, I learned so much about digital prototyping and also how to receive feedback and give feedback to other fellow students.

For this project i got 20 out of 50, it was very heartbreaking but it was okay. I was still proud of my app, I realized it was my first time and I struggled a lot to make it work. The feedback that I got from the lecturer was a bit superficial but it was still helpful. The lesson I learned from this project was, never stop trying and learn from your mistakes.
